Preparation of tea in few steps

Ingredients

cup of Water
2 Teaspoons of Sugar
Tea powder
1/2 cup of milk
lemon slice

Instructions

1.Boil the water
2.Place tea powder in a cup
3.Pour hot water over tea
4.Steep for 3-5 minutes
5.Add sugar or honey you prefer
6.Add milk if you prefer
7.Add lemon for flavour

Example Recipe
